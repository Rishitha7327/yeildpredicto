Under the Hood

The Technology Behind YeildPredicto

A multi-layer AI system built specifically for Indian agroclimate conditions.

🧠

Machine Learning Models

Ensemble of Random Forest, XGBoost, and Neural Network models trained on 13 years of district-level yield data from ICAR and state agriculture departments.

🛰️

Remote Sensing

NDVI and NDWI indices from ISRO Resourcesat-2 satellite imagery updated every 16 days — providing real-time crop health and moisture stress signals.

🌦️

Climate Modelling

IMD weather station data combined with ERA5 reanalysis for temperature, rainfall, humidity, and evapotranspiration forecasts at 1 km resolution.

🗺️

Geo-Spatial Analysis

District and village-level soil maps (NBSS&LUP) fused with GPS coordinates to identify the exact soil series, pH, and nutrient profile for your farm.
