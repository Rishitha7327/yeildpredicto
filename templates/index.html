Tips & Insights

Expert guidance from India's top agronomists.

Soil Health

Test soil pH regularly. Most crops thrive between pH 6.0–7.0. Add lime to raise pH or sulfur to lower it for optimal absorption and stronger yields.

Soil

Smart Irrigation

Drip irrigation saves 50% more water vs flood. Water early morning to cut evaporation. Moisture sensors prevent costly overwatering damage.

Water

Fertilizer Timing

Apply nitrogen in split doses — 1/3 at sowing, 1/3 at tillering, 1/3 at flowering. Improves uptake and reduces runoff by 35%.

Fertilizer

Crop Rotation

Rotate cereals with legumes every season. Legumes fix atmospheric nitrogen, cutting fertilizer cost by 30% for the next cereal crop.

Soil

Pest Management

IPM combines biological and chemical controls. Monitor weekly — a 10% infestation is far easier to control than 50%, saving your entire harvest.

Pest Control

Climate Adaptation

Drought-tolerant varieties for erratic rainfall regions. Mulching cuts soil temperature 5–8°C and retains 70% more moisture in peak summer.
