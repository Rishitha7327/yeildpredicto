Water Source Guide
🌧️ Rainfall
Relies on natural monsoon and seasonal precipitation. Best for kharif crops (June-Oct) and supplementary for rabi crops.
🚰 Well/Borewell
Underground groundwater accessed via wells. Provides year-round irrigation potential, ideal for year-round or rabi crops.
🌊 Canals/Channels
Irrigation water from government canals or water channels. Availability often seasonal; check with local irrigation department.
🏞️ Ponds/Tanks
Stored surface water from farm ponds or community tanks. Great for dry season crops but dependent on water accumulation.
🏞️ River/Stream
Proximity to flowing water sources enables direct irrigation. Best during monsoon and winter seasons.
💧 Drip/Micro System
Efficient irrigation systems using 30-50% less water. Ideal for high-value crops with precise water needs.
